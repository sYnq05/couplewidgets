Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

🔐 Short version: TwoUfUs is designed for privacy. We only collect the data strictly necessary to make the app work. Your location and notes are only shared with your connected partner – never with third parties or advertisers. Sync is powered by Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) infrastructure.

1. Who we are

TwoUfUs ("we", "us", "our") is an iOS application developed independently. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at jakobsworld@web.de.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the data necessary for the app's core features to function:

  • Apple ID / Sign in with Apple: TwoUfUs requires Sign in with Apple to create and manage your account. We store a stable Apple User Identifier (an anonymized ID provided by Apple) to link you and your partner. We do not receive your plain-text name or password.
  • Profile data: Your display name and initials (freely chosen by you), and your partner's display name and initials once connected.
  • Location data (optional): Your approximate or precise location, used solely to calculate and display the distance between you and your partner. Location data is shared only with your connected partner. With your consent, background location updates (significant location changes) may be used to keep the distance widget current even when the app is closed.
  • Shared note: A single shared note (up to 100 characters) you and your partner maintain together. Sending a new note replaces the previous one. You receive a push notification when your partner updates the note.
  • Countdown events: A date and optional label (up to 30 characters) for your shared countdown timer, synced between both devices.
  • Streak data: A rolling streak counter tracking consecutive days of activity between you and your partner.
  • Partner pairing data: An invite code (format XXXX-XXXX) used to establish the 1:1 connection between your Apple ID and your partner's Apple ID.

3. What data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your plain-text Apple ID email address or password (handled entirely by Apple).
  • We do not collect payment or financial data.
  • We do not use advertising networks or share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs that track your behavior across apps.

4. How we use your data

The data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating and identifying you via Sign in with Apple (Apple User Identifier).
  • Displaying the real-time distance between you and your partner on the Distance widget.
  • Delivering the shared note and sending a push notification to your partner when the note is updated.
  • Synchronizing the countdown event and streak data between paired devices.
  • Maintaining the pairing connection between your device and your partner's device.

5. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – Contract performance: Processing of your Apple User Identifier, profile data, shared note, countdown data, streak data, and pairing data is necessary to provide the core features of TwoUfUs.
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR – Consent: Processing of your location data is based on your explicit consent via iOS location permissions. You may withdraw consent at any time in your iPhone's Settings.
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – Legitimate interests: Basic technical security measures to protect the integrity of data transmitted between devices.

6. Infrastructure and data sharing

TwoUfUs uses Apple's infrastructure as its backend. This means your data is stored in and synced via iCloud (CloudKit Public Database). Apple acts as a data processor on our behalf. Additionally, Sign in with Apple and push notifications are provided by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies to these services: apple.com/privacy.

Your data is shared only with your connected partner. We do not share your data with any other third parties, except where required by applicable law.

7. Data retention

Your data remains in iCloud (CloudKit) as long as you use the app. If you sign out of the app, your data is not immediately deleted from iCloud – it may remain there for a limited period and can be reloaded if you sign back in with the same Apple ID. To permanently remove your data, please contact us at jakobsworld@web.de and we will initiate deletion from CloudKit within 30 days.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete your data from our CloudKit database.
  • Right to correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You may withdraw your consent to location processing at any time via iOS Settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at jakobsworld@web.de.

9. Signing out and data deletion

You can sign out of TwoUfUs at any time by navigating to Settings → Account → Sign Out within the app. Note that signing out does not immediately delete your data from iCloud – it may remain for a limited time and can be restored if you sign back in. To request permanent deletion of your data, contact us at jakobsworld@web.de.

10. Children's privacy

TwoUfUs is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

11. Location permissions

TwoUfUs may request access to your device's location to power the Distance widget. Location access is optional – if you decline, all other features remain available. You can control location permissions at any time in your iPhone's Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → TwoUfUs. Granting "Always" permission allows background location updates (significant location changes) so the distance stays current even when the app is closed. Granting "While Using" updates the distance only while the app is open. If you revoke location access, the Distance widget will stop showing a distance.

12. Push notifications

TwoUfUs uses CloudKit subscriptions to send push notifications to your partner's device when you update the shared note. You can disable these notifications at any time in Settings → Notifications → TwoUfUs on your iPhone.

13. Security

All data transmitted between devices is encrypted in transit. Data stored in iCloud benefits from Apple's security measures. We take appropriate organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Contact
