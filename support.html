Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a widget to my lock screen?▾
Long-press your iPhone lock screen, then tap the "Customize" button at the bottom. Select "Lock Screen", then tap the area where you want to add a widget. Scroll through the list to find TwoUfUs and tap to add it.
How do I connect with my partner?▾
Open TwoUfUs and tap "Connect Partner" on the home screen. You’ll get a unique invite code or link. Share it with your partner via any messaging app – once they enter the code in their app, you’ll be connected.
Why is the distance widget not showing?▾
Make sure location permissions are enabled for TwoUfUs. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → TwoUfUs and set it to "Always" or "While Using App". Also ensure your partner has done the same and that both of you are connected in the app.
My partner isn’t receiving my messages – what do I do?▾
Check that your partner has notifications enabled for TwoUfUs in Settings → Notifications → TwoUfUs. Also verify that both devices are connected to the internet, and that you’re still paired in the app. If the issue persists, try disconnecting and reconnecting in Settings within the app.
How do I create a countdown?▾
Open TwoUfUs and tap the "Countdown" tab. Tap the + button to create a new event, enter a name and date, and save. The countdown will automatically appear on both your and your partner’s widgets once they’re set up.
Is my location shared all the time?▾
Your location is only shared with your connected partner, never with us or anyone else. You can pause location sharing at any time within the app under Settings → Location → Pause Sharing. Your location is only used to calculate and display the distance widget.
How do I delete my account and data?▾
To delete your account, go to Settings within the TwoUfUs app and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data from our systems within 30 days. You can also uninstall the app to remove local data from your device.
Which iPhone models and iOS versions are supported?▾
TwoUfUs requires iOS 16 or later (for lock screen widget support). It is compatible with iPhone 8 and newer. For the best experience, we recommend running the latest version of iOS.
